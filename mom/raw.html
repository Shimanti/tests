Baked Potato Fillings

Spoon into potato.

Mix ½ cup chickpeas, 2 tbspn diced onions with chat masala, cilantro and lemon juice.

Broccoli
Steam one large bunch of broccoli. Set aside.
Dice 1 medium onion.
Mince 3-4 cloves garlic.
Dice 1 medium tomato.
Fry sliced green chilli (optional) in 1 tbspn oil.
Add onion. Fry till red.
Add garlic. Fry till aroma arises.
Add tomato, ½ tspn salt, 1 pkt sugar substitute. Fry slightly.
Add broccoli. Fry until dry.
Ingredients:
1 large bunch broccoli (cut into small pieces)
1 medium onion (diced)
3-4 cloves garlic (crushed)
1 medium tomato (diced)
1 green chilli (optional)
salt
sugar/sugar substitute

Cauliflower and Potatoes
Cut 2 medium potato into small pieces.
Cut 1 large cauliflower into small pieces.
Dice 1 large tomato.
Fry a pinch of kalogiray and sliced green chilli in 2 tbspn oil.
Add potatoes and fry till red. Cover.
Add 1 tspn tumeric and 2 pkt sugar substitute. Fry.
Add tomato. Fry.
Add cauliflower, peas and 1 tspn salt. Fry slightly.
Lower heat. Cover and simmer 15 mins until dry.
Add ½ cup water if needed and more salt/sugar to taste.
Before serving, add 1 tspn ground cumin, 1 tspn dried chilli, and 1 tspn garam masala.
Ingredients:
1 large cauliflower (chopped into small pieces)
2 medium potato (chopped into small pieces)
1 large tomato (diced)
pinch of kalogiray
1 green chilli (optional)
2 tbspn oil
1 tspn tumeric
2 pkts sugar substitute
1 tspn salt
water
1 tspn ground cumin
1 tspn dried chilli
1 tspn garam masala

Chicken Biryani
Chicken Mixture: Other Ingredients:
4 pieces chicken (washed) 1 medium onion (diced)
1 medium onion (diced) 1 ½ cups rice (washed/drained)
½ inch ginger (grated) 1 large potato (cut into 8)
4-5 cloves garlic (minced) whole garam masala
3 cups water 1 cup frozen peas (optional)
½ tspn salt ½ stick butter
1 pkt sugar substitute ½ tspn salt
1 tspn ground garam masala
¼ tspn peppercorns
In pressure cooker, boil all ingredients above.
Pressure cook for 3 mins. Or cook in rice cooker.
Set aside.
Dice 1 medium onion.
Wash and drain 1 ½ cups rice.
Chop 1 potato into 8 pieces (optional).
In pressure cooker, fry whole garam masala in 1 tbspn oil.
Add potato and onion. Fry till red.
Add rice, 1 cup frozen peas, ½ stick butter, ½ tspn salt. Fry slightly.
Add 3 cups of water from chicken mixture (double the rice portion - add water if needed
to make the 3 cups).
Add chicken mixture. Stir.
Add more salt/sugar to taste.
Pressure cook for 4 mins.
Before serving, add 1 tspn ground garam masala and ¼ tspn crushed peppercorns.

Eggplant in Yogurt
Cut l large eggplant in half lengthwise. Cut each half into four wedges.
Dice 1 medium onion.
Crush 4-5 cloves garlic.
Fry onion until red in 2 tbspn oil.
Add garlic. Fry till aroma arises.
Add eggplant and fry until golden over low flame.
Add ½ tspn tumeric, ½ tspn dried chilli, upto 1 cup water, ½ tspn salt, 1 packet sugar
substitute.
Cover and simmer until cooked.
Add 1 tbspn pickled masala and stir. Cook for 3 mins.
Add 2 tbspn beaten yogurt. Stir.
Taste and add salt/sugar as desired.
Ingredients:
1 large eggplant (cut into wedges)
1 medium onion (diced)
4-5 cloves garlic (crushed)
2 tbspn oil
½ tspn tumeric
½ tspn ground chilli
water
½ tspn salt
1 packet sugar substitute
1 tbspn pickle masala
2 tbspn plain yogurt

Eggplant Sauteed w/Egg
Cut eggplant into 8 slices. Wash in coriander, add pinch of salt, and sugar. Set aside.
Dice 1 medium onion. Mince 4-5 cloves garlic.
Fry a pinch of kalogiray and sliced green chilli in 2 tbspn oil.
Add onion. Fry till red.
Add garlic. Fry till aroma arises.
Add eggplant and ½ tspn tumeric.
Stir, cover and simmer for 10 mins.
(If dry, add water so that eggplant cooks.)
OR Pressure cook for 2 mins.
When slightly mushy, add ½ tspn salt, and stir.
When completely cooked and mushy, add beaten egg, cilantro, salt and sugar to taste.
Stir like crazy over high flame until dry.
Ingredients:
1 eggplant (soft inside)
1 medium onion (diced)
4-5 cloves garlic (minced)
pinch of kalogiray
1 green chilli (sliced)
2 tbspn oil
½ tspn tumeric
½ tspn salt
1 egg (beaten)
cilantro
salt/sugar to taste

Keechuri
Wash and strain ½ cup masoori dahl with ½ cup rice.
Set aside.
Dice 1 medium onion. Mince 4-5 cloves garlic.
In pressure cooker, fry whole garam masala, bay leaf and whole chilli in 1 tbspn oil.
When aroma arises, add ½ tspn whole cumin.
Add onion and garlic. Fry till golden.
Add 1 diced tomato, rice, dahl. Fry slightly.
Add 3 cups water, 1 cup frozen peas, other veg. (optional), 1 tspn salt, 1 pkt sugar
substitute.
Add chilli and butter if desired.
Pressure cook for 4 mins.
Before serving, add ½ tspn ground garam masala and cilantro.
Ingredients:
½ cup masoori dahl
½ cup basmati rice
1 medium onion (diced)
4-5 cloves garlic (crushed/minced)
whole garam masala
bay leaf
1 green chilli (sliced)
1 tbspn oil
½ tspn whole cumin
1 medium tomato (diced)
1 cup frozen peas, other veg. (optional)
salt
sugar/sugar substitute
ground chilli, butter (optional)
ground garam masala
fresh cilantro (sliced)
Indian Batter for Frying Vegetables
1 cup gram flour
(or 1 cup all purpose flour plus 1 tbspn oil)
½ cup water (or slightly less)
½ tspn kalogiray
pinch of salt
Mix all ingredients together. Add water slowly to form droopy consistency.
Wash vegetables (cauliflower, carrots, broccoli, eggplant, etc.) Slice into moderate
pieces, and mix with pinch of salt. Set aside.
Dip vegetables in batter and deep fry.

Lamb Curry
Soak 2 lbs of lamb (shank cut into small pieces) in ½ cup white vinegar overnight.
Lamb marinade:
Blend ½ cup yogurt, 2 diced onions, 7-8 cloves diced garlic, and 1 inch grated ginger in
liquidizer.
Set mixture aside.
Wash lamb in warm water, throwing away vinegar.
In large bowl, mix lamb with yogurt mixture, 2 tbspns oil, 1 tspn tumeric, 1 tspn ground
cumin, 1 tspn ground chilli.
Set aside.
Dice 1 large tomato.
Dice 1 large onion.
Crush 3-4 cloves garlic
In pressure cooker, fry whole garam masala and 1 bay leaf in 2 tbspn oil.
Add onion, garlic, 1 packet sugar.
When red, add tomato and fry slightly.
When oily, add lamb mixture, 1 tspn salt. Fry slightly.
Put on pressure when water starts coming out. Pressure for 10 mins.
(If adding potatoes and carrots, pressure for 6 mins, add 2 potatoes cut into big pieces
and 2 chopped carrots. Cool and pressure again for 4 mins.)
Before removing, if watery, fry until sauce dries slightly. Taste, add salt/sugar if desired.
Set aside for 10 mins. Throw away excess oil that comes to the surface.

Mustard Sauce (for fish)
Soak ½ cup mustard seeds in ½ cup plus 1 inch of water overnight.
Drain over tea strainer.
Place mustard seeds in blender with water that exceeds the seeds by 1 inch.
Add 1 tbspn oil, ½ tspn salt, 2 whole green chillis.
Blend until smooth.
Put in small bottles and freeze.
Ingredients:
½ cup mustard seeds
water
1 tbspn oil
½ tspn salt
2 whole green chillis

Shrimp and Potato Curry
Chop 2 potatoes into 8 pieces. Fry and set aside.
Dice 1 large onion.
Crush 4-5 cloves garlic.
Grate ½ inch ginger.
In pressure cooker, fry whole garam masala and 1 bay leaf until red in 2 tbspn oil.
Add onion. Fry till red.
Add garlic and ginger. Fry till aroma arises.
Add ½ tspn tumeric, ½ tspn ground cumin, ½ tspn dried chilli. Fry slightly with a little
water.
Add ½ cup water, potatoes, ½ lb shrimp, ½ tspn salt, ½ pkt sugar substitute. Taste
Boil, cover and simmer for 10 mins.
OR pressure for 2 mins.
Ingredients:
½ lb shrimp
2 medium potatoes (cut into 8 pieces)
1 large onion (diced)
4-5 cloves garlic (crushed)
½ inch ginger (grated)
whole garam masala
bay leaf
2 tbspn oil
½ tspn turmeric
½ tspn ground cumin
½ tspn dried chilli
water
½ tspn salt
½ pkt sugar substitute

Tandori Chicken
Ingredients:
4 pieces chicken
2 tbspn plain yogurt
1 tbspn “Rajah” tandori mix
1 large onion (diced)
3-4 cloves garlic (minced)
Mix all ingredients and marinate overnight.
Heat oven at 400 degrees.
Line roasting dish with foil and spray oil.
Place chicken on foil with marinate and cover with foil.
Roast 20 mins. Turn, marinate with lemon juice and roast another 20 mins without
cover. Grill if desired.

Trout in Mustard Sauce
Wash 2 fillets of trout. Marinate in ½ tspn tumeric and ½ tspn salt for 15 mins.
In frying pan, spray oil and fry each fillet. Set aside.
(Optional) Chop 1 medium potato into wedges. Fry and set aside.
Dice 1 small onion.
Chop 1 small tomato into wedges.
Fry sliced green chilli in 1 tbspn oil.
Add onion. Fry till red.
Add tomato, ½ tspn tumeric. Fry slightly.
Add ½ cup water, potatoes (optional - add more water if needed) and boil.
Cover and simmer.
When potatoes are half-boiled, add 2 tbspn mustard sauce and fish.
Cover and simmer for 10 mins or until moderately dry.
Ingredients:
1 trout (cleaned and filleted)
½ tspn tumeric
½ tspn salt
oil
1 medium potato (cut into wedges) (optional)
1 small onion (diced)
1 small tomato (cut into wedges)
1 green chilli (sliced)
½ tspn tumeric
½ cup water
2 tbspn mustard sauce

Trout Curry (Bengali style)
Wash 2 fillets of trout. Marinate in ½ tspn tumeric and ½ tspn salt for 15 mins.
In frying pan, spray oil and fry each fillet. Set aside.
Dice 1 medium onion.
Chop 1 medium tomato into wedges.
Cut 1 green pepper into this strips (optional).
Fry sliced green chilli (optional) in 1 tbspn oil.
Add onion. Fry till red.
Add tomato, ½ tspn tumeric. Fry slightly.
Add ½ cup water and boil.
Add fish. Cover with bell peppers and add salt to taste.
Cover and simmer for 10 mins.
Before serving, add cilantro.
Ingredients:
1 trout (cleaned and filleted)
½ tspn tumeric
½ tspn salt
oil
1 medium onion (diced)
1 medium tomato (cut into wedges)
1 green chilli (sliced) (optional)
1 green pepper (cut into strips) (optional)
½ tspn tumeric
½ cup water
cilantro

Tuna Kebabs
Bake 2 potatoes in microwave.
Peel and mash with tuna mixture above.
Add salt, lemon juice, cilantro to taste.
Shape into flat rounds. (Freeze if desired)
Beat 1 egg for every 6 kebabs.
Dip kebabs in egg.
Dip in breadcrumbs.
Fry and serve.

Turkey Kebab
½ cup chana dahl
1lb minced turkey
3 small diced onions
1 inch ginger (grated)
6-7 cloves garlic (minced)
1 tspn peppercorns (crushed)
½ packet “shan” (shami kebab mix)
1 cup water
1 tspn salt
cilantro
In pressure cooker, boil all ingredients above.
Pressure cook for 5 mins.
When cool, mash all ingredients over cooker.
Add lemon juice, ground chilli, and cilantro to taste.
Shape into flat rounds. (Freeze if desired)
Beat 1 egg for every 6 kebabs.
Dip kebabs in egg.
Fry and serve.
